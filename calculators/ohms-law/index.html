Engineering Notes

  • 4-band resistors are commonly used for general-purpose applications.
  • 5-band resistors provide higher precision using three significant digits.
  • 6-band resistors include a temperature coefficient for precision circuits.
  • Gold and Silver bands are valid only as multiplier and tolerance bands.
