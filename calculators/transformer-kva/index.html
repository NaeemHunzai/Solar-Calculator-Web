Engineering Notes

  • Select the next higher standard transformer rating above the calculated capacity.
  • Future expansion helps avoid premature transformer replacement.
  • Continuous transformer loading is generally recommended between 70% and 80%.
  • High ambient temperature and installation altitude may require transformer derating.
  • Verify primary and secondary voltages before selecting the transformer.
