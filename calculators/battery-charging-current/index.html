Engineering Notes

  • Charging time is estimated using battery capacity, charging current and charging efficiency.
  • Charging efficiency is automatically selected based on the battery chemistry.
  • Lead-acid batteries require an absorption stage near full charge, increasing total charging time.
  • Lithium batteries typically use Constant Current (CC) followed by Constant Voltage (CV) charging.
  • Actual charging time may vary depending on battery age, temperature and charger performance.
  • For best battery life, always use the manufacturer's recommended charging current.
