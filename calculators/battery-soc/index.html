Engineering Notes

  • Voltage-based SOC estimation is most accurate when the battery has been at rest for at least 30 minutes.
  • Remaining Capacity and Used Capacity methods generally provide more accurate SOC estimation than voltage-based calculations.
  • Temperature affects battery voltage, especially for lead-acid batteries.
  • LiFePO₄ batteries have a very flat voltage curve, therefore voltage alone cannot accurately determine SOC.
  • Results are engineering estimates and should not replace measurements from a Battery Management System (BMS).
