Engineering Notes

  • Use the required reactive power (kVAR) obtained from a Power Factor Correction study or system design.
  • For three-phase systems, the selected connection (Delta or Star) affects the required capacitance per phase.
  • A safety margin helps compensate for capacitor ageing and future load growth.
  • Select a capacitor voltage rating higher than the operating system voltage.
  • Use detuned capacitor banks where harmonic distortion is significant.
